# Chapter 01: Project Overview

## 1.1 Platform Identity
Hospyn 2.0 (Advanced Health Platform) is an Enterprise-Grade Artificial Intelligence ecosystem designed for the medical sector. It operates as a high-throughput processing hub for Protected Health Information (PHI).

## 1.2 Mission and Strategic Goals
- **Mission:** To eliminate the manual burden of medical record review through sub-10 second AI summarization.
- **Goal 1: Absolute Privacy.** End-to-end encryption of PII so that data is unreadable in the database.
- **Goal 2: High Availability.** Self-healing architecture that recovers from server crashes automatically.
- **Goal 3: Real-time Feedback.** Instant WebSocket notifications for clinical analysis completion.

## 1.3 Core Features Functional Breakdown
- **AI-Native OCR:** Extracting clinical data from messy, handwritten, or scanned documents.
- **Unified AI Engine:** A fallback-aware engine that switches between Gemini 1.5 Pro, Groq (Llama 3), and Anthropic based on latency and accuracy.
- **Physician Dashboard:** A premium web view with aggregated analytics.
- **Patient Portal:** A mobile-native experience for record management and chat.
