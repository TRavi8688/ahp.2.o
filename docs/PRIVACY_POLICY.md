# Hospyn Privacy Policy
**Last Updated: May 9, 2026**

## 1. Introduction
Hospyn ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your medical and personal data. This policy explains how we handle Health Information under global standards (including HIPAA and GDPR-style protections).

## 2. Data We Collect
- **Health Records**: Scans, vitals, prescriptions, and lab reports uploaded by you or your healthcare provider.
- **Identity Data**: Name, DOB, and biometric hashes (for secure login).
- **Organization Data**: Professional licenses (for partners) and hospital identifiers.

## 3. Data Usage & Sharing
- **Zero-Sale Policy**: We **never** sell your health data to third parties.
- **Provider Access**: Your data is only shared with hospitals and partners **after you scan their QR code** or provide explicit consent.
- **AI Verification**: We use AI to verify medical licenses for the safety of the network.

## 4. Security
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3).
- **Tenant Isolation**: Your data is cryptographically locked to your unique identity.

## 5. Data Deletion
Users have the right to request full account deletion. This can be initiated directly within the **Hospyn Patient** app under 'Settings > Account Safety'.

## 6. Contact
For privacy inquiries, contact: `privacy@hospyn.com`
